Targeted Platforms:

1- iPhone

2- iPad

3- Android

4- Desktop Chrome

5- Desktop Safari

 

Requirements:

Manage you code through Single Tone design pattern.

 

Output:

Display 5 circles at the screen, fading in at center and then 4 of them nicely slides towards the four edges of the screen. Center of all of the four circles should be aligned with the edges of the screen.

 

Size of each circle should be 10% of the screen width.

 

When user rollover each of the four circles, it should jump to the opposite corner of the screen along height.

 

Note: Changing the orientation of the screen or resizing the browser should readjust the positions of circles as per new dimensions with a nice animation.
